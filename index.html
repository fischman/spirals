🌀 Integers on Spirals

Plot natural numbers along different spiral paths and highlight selected subsets in hopes of finding non-obvious patterns.

Controls:

1–7  switch spiral type
⇧1–⇧7  switch highlighted subset
Mouse Scroll on canvas to zoom in/out
Mouse Hover a point to see its index
