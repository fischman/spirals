🌀 Integers on Spirals

Plot natural numbers along different spiral paths and highlight selected subsets in hopes of finding non-obvious patterns.

Controls:

1–8  switch spiral type
⇧1–⇧8  switch highlighted subset
?  open this help
Scroll / Pinch on canvas to zoom in/out
Hover a point to see its index
